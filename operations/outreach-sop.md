# RankLift Local Outreach SOP

## Daily rules
- Use free tools only.
- Target USA small-town service contractors.
- Send only personalized outreach, not bulk spam.
- Daily starting volume: 10–20 messages.
- Respect opt-outs immediately.
- Do not claim guaranteed rankings or revenue.
- Do not spend money or subscribe to paid tools without user approval.

## Target niches
1. Septic tank cleaning / septic pumping
2. Mobile RV repair
3. Gutter cleaning
4. Stump grinding
5. Garage door repair
6. Junk removal

## Lead qualification
Prefer businesses with at least one of:
- weak/old website
- low review count vs nearby competitor
- no clear service keywords
- no obvious recent Google activity
- missing/weak service descriptions
- contact email or website contact form available
